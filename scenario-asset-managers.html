Method

One engine, multiple governance lenses

GSAT evaluates governance performance across five domains and translates structural conditions into score, resilience classification, and transmission risk. The simulator demonstrates output behavior while preserving proprietary model calibration.

Independent benchmark

GSAT measures governance performance under stress rather than certifying compliance against a narrow rule set.

Stress deterioration view

Baseline and stressed conditions are shown together so users can see how governance worsens under pressure.

Scenario overlays

The same engine can be applied to different participant contexts without changing the visual product language.

Protected methodology

The public simulator is intentionally illustrative. Full supervisory calibration remains inside engagement.
